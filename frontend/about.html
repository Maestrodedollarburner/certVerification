About CertiChain

A blockchain-based solution for academic certificate verification

Problem Statement

Educational institutions continue to face challenges related to certificate forgery, document tampering, and inefficient verification processes. Traditional certificate verification methods are time-consuming and vulnerable to manipulation.

CertiChain provides a secure blockchain-based platform where academic certificates can be issued, recorded, and verified with confidence.

Technology Stack

Frontend

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap 5

Backend

Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB

Blockchain

Ethereum, Solidity, Ganache, Web3.js

Security

bcrypt, JWT, SHA-256 Hashing

Blockchain Principles Demonstrated

  • Decentralization — Distributed ledger with no single point of control
  • Transparency — All transactions visible and auditable
  • Immutability — Records cannot be altered once written
  • Security — Cryptographic hashing protects data integrity
  • Traceability — Complete history from issuance to verification
